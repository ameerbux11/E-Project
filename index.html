Hi, I'm FURRO.
During my service, I met incredible dogs. Despite the harsh weather, long missions, and solitude — their presence gave us strength. Army dogs weren’t just support — they became our family.

Rex, the Lifesaver.
Rex, one of our smartest K9s, saved lives countless times. His loyalty and courage during every mission formed a bond stronger than anything I’ve known. It was more than duty — it was love.

“These dogs were not pets. They were heroes. Their loyalty shaped who we became.”

After my service, I began researching dog breeds we worked with. With my friend Chad — a vet and dog lover — we launched Furro to share our knowledge and honor our brave companions.

Thank you for visiting Furro.
We invite you to explore these stories, learn about loyal breeds, and help celebrate the silent heroes of service.
